MakerVault uses a simple, fair licensing model. Pay once, keep what you have.

## Free trial

MakerVault includes a **7-day free trial** with full functionality. No credit card required. Just download and start using it.

A subtle banner shows how many days remain in your trial.

## Purchasing

MakerVault is a one-time purchase:

- **$39** early bird (first 200 buyers)
- **$59** standard price

Optional annual updates are **$20/year** for major version updates (v1.1, v1.2, v2.0). If you stop paying for updates, you keep the version you have — it still works, you just don't get new features.

## Activating your license

1. Purchase MakerVault to receive your license key (format: `MV-XXXX-XXXX-XXXX`).
2. Open MakerVault and go to the activation screen.
3. Enter your license key and click **Activate**.
4. MakerVault validates your key with the license server and stores a signed token locally.

## Offline use

After initial activation (which requires an internet connection), MakerVault works fully offline. The signed license token is stored locally and validated without contacting the server.

## Device limit

Each license can be activated on up to **2 devices**. The activation screen shows how many devices are currently using your license.

To move your license to a new device:

1. Open MakerVault on the old device.
2. Go to Settings and click **Deactivate License**.
3. Activate on the new device.

## License status

Your license status is visible in Settings, showing:

- Email associated with the license
- Number of devices used / allowed
- Update subscription expiry date
- License key

## Keep what you have

MakerVault follows the same model as LightBurn: if you stop paying for updates, the version you have continues to work indefinitely. You own your copy.
