title: Installation
description: Download and install MakerVault on your Mac.
---

MakerVault is available for macOS 10.15 (Catalina) and later. Windows support is planned for v1.1.

## Download

1. Visit [makervaultapp.com](https://makervaultapp.com) and click **Download**.
2. Open the downloaded `.dmg` file.
3. Drag MakerVault to your **Applications** folder.

## First launch

When you first open MakerVault, macOS may show a security prompt since the app was downloaded from the internet. Click **Open** to proceed.

MakerVault creates a local SQLite database at:

```
~/Library/Application Support/com.makervault.app/makervault.db
```

All your data stays on your computer. Nothing is sent to the cloud.

## System requirements

| Requirement | Minimum |
|---|---|
| **OS** | macOS 10.15 (Catalina) |
| **Disk space** | ~5 MB for the app |
| **RAM** | 256 MB |

The app is lightweight thanks to Tauri's native webview architecture. It uses your system's built-in WebKit instead of bundling a browser engine.

## Free trial

MakerVault includes a 7-day free trial with full functionality. No credit card required. After the trial, you can purchase a license to continue using the app.

## Updates

MakerVault checks for updates automatically. When an update is available, you'll see a notification in the app. Updates are downloaded and installed in the background.
